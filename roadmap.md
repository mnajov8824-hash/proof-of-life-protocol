# Roadmap

## Phase 1 — MVP (0–3 months)
Goal: Prove that the idea works in real life.

- Written protocol (done)
- Manual verification process
- Small pilot group (10–50 animals)
- Monthly payments tested
- Feedback from animal owners
- Adjust payment size and rules

## Phase 2 — Pilot Expansion (3–12 months)
Goal: Scale carefully and reduce manual work.

- Simple web app for video submission
- Reviewer dashboard
- Community-based verification
- First NGO or donor partnerships
- Public transparency reports
- First 1,000 animals protected

## Phase 3 — Global Protocol (1–3 years)
Goal: Make animal preservation economically competitive worldwide.

- Decentralized verification
- Smart contract–based payments
- Multi-country deployment
- Partnerships with governments and NGOs
- Millions of animals protected
- Proof of Life as a global standard
